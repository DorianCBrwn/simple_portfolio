The Process

Lets work together to create unqiue and effective web experiences.

Every website has a unqiue and special story. By folowing this 4 part process I can ensure your website will have maximum impact and tell the story you envision.

  • Consultation

    Every website is personalized to your unqiue business needs so we start with a conversation. You can explain your needs, wants and what is most important to you.

  • Proposal

    Next, you will be sent a detailed plan of action that will layout all of the deliverables and expectations for your project.

  • Creation

    After the proposal has been sent and the contract signed, work will being on your website. To keep you up to date, you will receive weekly project updates enabling you to track progress and make necessary changes before the final build.

  • Launch

    After final approval the project will go live and I will provide support through out the entire launch phase.
